This site undertakes the important task of cataloguing and ranking every level in Spyro 2: Gateway to Glimmer. The metrics for ranking are detailed below.

Before being given a final score, each level is ranked over three aspects:

Aesthetics

This includes visual design, character design, music and sounds. Bonus points for an excellent skybox.

Setting

This includes the "story" of the level: the background of the world, Spyro's quests, and the narrative aspects of the character design.

Gameplay

What is the gameplay like in this level? Are the side-quests repetitive and frustrating, or exciting and well constructed?
